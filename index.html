Our Mission

Empowering youth to lead and make a difference in the city of Largo, Florida.

About the council

The Largo Youth Leadership Council is an organization within the city of Largo, Florida. We are dedicated to empowering 8th-12th grade students with an opportunity to lead and make a difference in their community to participate in leadership and community service to help make a difference in the city of Largo.
